About

Mochi Island is a Fantasy text turn based RPG game, where you play as one of the leaders of the monchi clan and your goal is to stop the Corrupted Monchi Leader from consuming you and your people. While fighting the boss you will be able to scavenge for various items within the area, from various weapons left behind by the fallen warriors of the past to mysterious berries that gives you positive or negative effects. Do eat them at your own risk. But beware the boss has a special attack, and is also able to obtain mysterious berries by sucking the area.

Mochi Island is an island inhabited by mochis high in the sky. After years and years of fighting the area in which the mochis had their ferocious battles flew up high in the sky and was separated into 3 different islands. None of the mochis know how and why this happened and probably did not care due to how the only thing they cared for atbthe time was destroying each other. As the mochi leaders declared a truce with each other, they unanimously agreed to build a new civalization ontop of the flying islands. There the mochis from the 5 clans live in harmony among each other

Character

Mochi Is the leader of one of the 5 mochi clans. A kind, compassionate, polite and has the heart of a child. He may seem like a vounrable creature that can be killed in a snap but don't underestimate him for he is one of the most fearsome and strongest warriors in his clan. Him and his clan are neutral mochis who finds the whole war between the other 5 clans to be pointless but wouldn't hesitate to fight back when it involves their clan being endangered, so when they heard that the other 4 clans are calling for a truce they where relieved and happy, Mochi especially. But little did he and his clan know that a greater storm is coming for them.

The mochi king is one of the leaders of the 5 mochi clans, one who thrives off of war and believes that winning is everything, cared for the mochis who fought with and alongside him and believes that you can't settle a problem by making peace with your enemies. He didn't agree with the peace treaty and vows to consume the other leaders to finish their conflict and to justify the deaths of his fallen mochis
